Our story

Built by people who hate dead batteries.

Compact started with a missed flight. A dead phone. A boarding pass we couldn't pull up. We looked at every powerbank on the shelf and none of them were the one we'd actually carry.

So we built it. Small enough to disappear in a jacket pocket. Powerful enough to charge a MacBook on a red-eye. Smart enough to know what device is plugged in and deliver exactly the current it wants — no more, no less.

Every Compact goes through a six-stage SafeCharge protocol before it leaves the factory. Every unit is BIS certified. Every claim we make is measured, not marketed.
